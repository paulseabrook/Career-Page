If you happened to be curious and/or nerdy enough to click the link, below are a few of my faves on the disc golf course.

  • Bag

    GRIPeq CX1. Comfortable, durable, lightweight, and has all the right pockets.

    GRIPeq CX1
  • Drivers

      Potentially my favorite disc off all time. The great Nate Sexton's go to forehand disc. Overstable, reliable, predictable, deadly.
      Innova Champion Glow Firebird Nate Sexton

      For a little more distance on my forehand shots, the destroyer is the perfect disc with a nice, stable finish. Innova Star Destroyer

  • Mid-ranges

      Extremely straight and dependable, the Discraft groove top Buzzz is my go to for backhand line-drive, stable finishes. Depending on your preference, the GT track on top can provide an added layer of grip and comfort. Discraft Buzzz GT

      An excellent mid-range and approach disc, I reach for this when I need a bit more distance for approach than my pig could provide. Also, a great flip-up forehand disc for drives if thrown on a hyzer.
      Innova Mako3

  • Putters

      The Innova R-Pro Pig is a great overstable putter and approach disc. Too far for me to jump putt comfortably? I can thorow this forehand or backhand with ease and predictability.
      Innova R-Pro Pig

      My go to putter, the Discraft Jawbreaker GT Banger is one of the best putting putters that I have used so far. The weight and feel of the plastic are excellent while the groove top provides a perfect grip.
      Banger GT
